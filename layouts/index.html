Me figuring out life.

When I started to travel the world as a digital nomad, I thought everything would be different. New locations, new people, new experiences, new me. But soon I realized that this wasn't the case at all.

Yes, everything was different, but still, I was the same. And I realized that I wasn't the only one. A lot of fellow nomads felt the same way. But it's not only a problem for us travelers; it's an issue for everyone. We all try to do things to distract us from the main problem: ourselves.

And until we focus on us and figure out what we want our life to be, we keep running and hiding.

So I make it my mission to work out my life and help others along the way.
